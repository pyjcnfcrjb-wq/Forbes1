## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse a Category">
    Use the sidebar or the cards above to pick a topic area that interests you. Each category is divided into clearly labeled subtopics, so you can find the right starting point whether you're a complete beginner or looking to fill in specific gaps in your knowledge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a Topic">
    Already have something specific in mind? Type any keyword, concept, or question into the **search bar** at the top of the page. Search covers every article across all categories and surfaces the most relevant results instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dive Deep with Further Reading Links">
    Every article includes links to related pages and, where applicable, a curated list of further reading. Follow these connections to build a well-rounded understanding of any subject — you can go as broad or as focused as your curiosity takes you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  New topics and articles are added to the knowledge base on a regular basis. Check back often to find fresh content, and visit the [Contributing](/contributing) page if you'd like to suggest a topic or help expand existing articles.
</Note>
